Jordan 4 'Pine Green'

$225.00 USD

 

The Nike SB x Air Jordan 4 Pine Green is based on the Air Jordan 4 silhouette, which was first released in 1989 and designed by Tinker Hatfield. The Nike SB x Air Jordan 4 Pine Green follows the same color blocking as some of the OG colorways of the Air Jordan 4, such as the “Military Blue” and the “Fire Red”. It features a white leather upper with a light grey suede mudguard that wraps around the toe and heel. The sneaker also has contrasting green accents on the eyelets, tongue lining, heel tab, and midsole.
