About Us

 

They say life is a game, and for me, that game was basketball. From the moment I picked up that ball, it was like I found my escape hatch from reality. The court became my sanctuary, a place where I could leave the troubles of the world behind and let the rhythm of the game dictate my steps. And as I grew older, I realized that I wasn't alone in this sentiment. There were others out there who felt the same way, who sought refuge in the game, who longed for that sense of belonging and purpose. That's when it struck me: I could create a community, a haven for those who, like me, found solace in the court's embrace. A place where we can be who we are and do whatever we want.
