Jordan 1 'Reimagined Royals'

$180.00 USD

 

The Air Jordan 1 Retro High OG is known to be made up of smooth leather panels, but Jordan designers instead opted to construct its side panels and toe boxes with buttery suede. Its “Royal Blue” accents land in its usual spots across the lower heel overlays, collars, Swooshes and toe boxes, the quarters and surrounding overlays are dialed to a solid black hue. Tongues retain their nylon material and are topped off with Nike Air branding. Customary white midsoles and blue treading are situated down below.
